How to Watch & Download Anime

Video Tutorial Guide

1

Find Your Anime And Choose The Video Quality

Browse through or use the search function to quickly find the anime you want to watch. Click on the download button then choose the video quality you want to get started.

2

Wait for Link Generation (don't refresh the page)

Click or close the ad and wait for the timer to end

3

Scroll Down And Find Get Link Button

Scroll down and find the "Get Link" button, click on it to open the video list. Select the video you want to watch (coming soon) or download.

4

Download

The Download Link Will be Generated Automatically. For downloading:

  1. Complete any required verification step (this helps us prevent abuse)
  2. Wait a moment while we prepare your download link
  3. Click the download button to save the file to your device
  4. Enjoy your anime offline anytime!
5

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using our service:

  • Download link not working? Ensure you've completed all verification steps and that your ad blocker is disabled for our site
  • Slow download speed? Try downloading during non-peak hours (early morning or late night) or check your internet connection
  • Subtitle issues? Make sure subtitles are enabled in the player settings and try selecting a different subtitle track if available
  • Mobile playback problems? Try using our progressive web app for better performance on mobile devices
