Synopsis

Zhou Yuan, the prince of the Great Zhou Empire, was born with a supreme dragon vein that was stolen by the Empress Wu Yao when he was just a baby. Stripped of his royal status and cultivation potential, Zhou Yuan is forced to grow up in hardship and adversity.

With the help of his mentor Cang Yuan and the mysterious Yaoyao, Zhou Yuan embarks on a journey to reclaim his stolen destiny. Through countless trials and battles, he gradually unlocks his true potential and uncovers the secrets of his lineage. Dragon Prince Yuan follows Zhou Yuan's path to becoming the strongest cultivator and reclaiming his rightful place in the world.
