Synopsis

Ten thousand years after Tang San's ascent to Godhood, the Tang Sect has declined. The only survivors are the sect's disciples who were sent into the outer sea. The continent has undergone tremendous changes during this time, and the four empires that once stood have been replaced by two: the Sun Moon Empire and the Star Luo Empire.

The story follows Huo Yuhao, a young boy who was born with a mutation spirit - the Spirit Eyes - that makes him appear weak. After encountering the million-year-old Skydream Iceworm in the forest, his fate changes. He enters Shrek Academy, meets new friends, and begins his journey to become the strongest spirit master and revive the Tang Sect.
