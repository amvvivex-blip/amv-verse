Synopsis

Marvel's First Family returns in this exciting reboot of the Fantastic Four franchise. The film follows four intrepid explorers who gain incredible abilities after their spacecraft is bombarded by cosmic rays during a scientific mission to outer space.

Reed Richards (Mr. Fantastic) gains the ability to stretch his body, Sue Storm (Invisible Woman) can become invisible and create force fields, Johnny Storm (Human Torch) can control fire and fly, and Ben Grimm (The Thing) possesses superhuman strength and a rock-like exterior. Together, they must learn to master their new powers and work as a team to stop the mysterious threat known as Doctor Doom.
