How to Submit a Takedown Request

To report specific infringing content, your DMCA notice must include:

  1. Your authorized signature (physical or electronic)
  2. Exact title and episode number of the infringing content
  3. Complete URL(s) where the content appears on our site
  4. Proof of copyright ownership (registration number or equivalent)
  5. Your contact information including legal address
  6. The statement: "I request removal of ONLY the specified content and understand that blanket demands for site removal are invalid under this policy."

Note: Generic notices requesting "all anime content" or "entire series" will be rejected. Specify exact infringing URLs.
